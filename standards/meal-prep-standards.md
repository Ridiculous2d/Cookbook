## Recipe Goals

---

---
Every recipe should be:

- High protein
- Whole-food focused
- Meal prep friendly
- Freezer friendly (when appropriate)
- Easy to follow
- Beginner friendly
- Version controlled

---

## Serving Guidelines

Target per serving:

- Calories: 450–550
- Protein: 35–40 g
- Fiber: 8–12 g
