# Ingredients

## Protein

- 1 lb boneless skinless chicken breast

## Grain Blend

- ½ cup uncooked brown rice
- ¼ cup uncooked quinoa

## Beans

- 1 (15 oz) can chickpeas, drained and rinsed

## Vegetables

- 1 (12 oz) bag frozen broccoli florets

## Fresh Toppings

- 3 cups baby spinach (1 cup per serving)
- 1 medium cucumber, sliced
- 1½ cups shredded carrots (½ cup per serving)
- 3 oz feta cheese (1 oz per serving)
- 6 tbsp light lemon vinaigrette (2 tbsp per serving)

## Chicken Seasoning

- 1 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp Italian seasoning
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 1 tsp lemon pepper
- Salt
- Pepper

## Broccoli Seasoning

- Olive oil spray
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- Salt
- Pepper

---

# Prep Order

- Gather ingredients.
- Preheat oven to **425°F**.
- Start brown rice.
- Season chicken.
- Bake chicken.
- Start quinoa.
- Roast broccoli.
- Prep fresh toppings.
- Drain and rinse chickpeas.
- Allow cooked ingredients to cool.
- Assemble freezer bags.

---

# Sunday Meal Prep

1. Gather all ingredients and equipment.

2. Preheat oven to **425°F**.

3. Start the brown rice according to package directions.

4. Pat the chicken dry. Toss with olive oil, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, lemon pepper, salt, and pepper until evenly coated.

5. Place the chicken on a sheet pan and bake for **20–25 minutes**, or until the internal temperature reaches **165°F**.

6. Once the rice is cooking, start the quinoa according to package directions.

7. Toss the frozen broccoli with olive oil spray, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Spread onto a sheet pan and roast for **20–25 minutes**, stirring halfway through.

8. While the broccoli roasts:
   - Slice the cucumber.
   - Portion the spinach.
   - Portion the shredded carrots.
   - Portion the feta cheese.

9. Drain and rinse the chickpeas.

10. Allow the chicken, rice, quinoa, and broccoli to cool for **20–30 minutes** before packaging.

---

# Assemble Freezer Bags

Place the following into **one** freezer bag:

- ⅓ of the cooked chicken
- ⅓ of the cooked brown rice
- ⅓ of the cooked quinoa
- ⅓ of the chickpeas
- ⅓ of the roasted broccoli

Repeat for all **3** servings.

---

# Refrigerate

Divide into **3** portions:

- 1 cup baby spinach
- ⅓ of the sliced cucumber
- ½ cup shredded carrots
- 1 oz feta cheese
- 2 tbsp light lemon vinaigrette

---

# Meal Day

### The Night Before

Move one freezer bag from the freezer to the refrigerator.

### Heat

Microwave the freezer bag contents for **2–3 minutes**, stirring halfway through, until heated through.

### Finish

Top with:

- 1 cup baby spinach
- ⅓ of the sliced cucumber
- ½ cup shredded carrots
- 1 oz feta cheese
- 2 tbsp light lemon vinaigrette
